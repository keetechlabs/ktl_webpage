Effective Date: March 31, 2025

Last Updated: March 31, 2025

KeetechLabs Private Limited (“KeetechLabs,” “Company,” “We,” “Us,” or “Our”) is committed to protecting the privacy and security of our clients, users, and stakeholders. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, store, and disclose personal and business-related data in compliance with applicable laws and industry best practices.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information

  • Full name, email address, contact number
  • Billing details, payment method information (excluding sensitive financial data)
  • Business name, company registration details (if applicable)

1.2 Technical & Usage Information

  • IP address, browser type, operating system, and device details
  • Login credentials (hashed and encrypted for security)
  • User activity logs, including website interactions, app usage, and service requests
  • Cookies and tracking technologies for enhanced user experience

1.3 Third-Party Integrations

  • Information retrieved from third-party platforms such as Firebase, payment gateways, hosting services, and APIs integrated within our solutions
  • Any additional data required for functionality, support, or legal compliance

2. How We Use the Collected Information

  • Providing and maintaining our services
  • Processing transactions and managing billing
  • Enhancing customer support and troubleshooting
  • Communicating important updates, security alerts, and service-related notifications
  • Improving our services, websites, and mobile applications
  • Enforcing contractual agreements, including payment collection
  • Complying with legal obligations and law enforcement requests

3. Data Storage & Security Measures

  • Encryption: Sensitive data is encrypted at rest and in transit.
  • Access Control: Only authorized personnel have access to specific data.
  • Regular Audits: Security assessments and compliance checks are conducted periodically.
  • Secure Hosting: We use industry-leading data centers with robust security standards.

4. Data Sharing & Third-Party Access

  • With service providers and third-party vendors for operational purposes (e.g., payment processing, hosting, email services)
  • For legal compliance, regulatory requirements, or law enforcement requests
  • In case of business transfers, mergers, or acquisitions where your data may be transferred to a new entity with continued privacy safeguards

5. Data Retention Policy

  • We retain client data as long as the subscription is active.
  • Upon subscription cancellation, data is stored for 60 days at no cost.
  • Post 60 days, the client must pay ₹590/month for continued storage.
  • If a client requests deletion of their data, we comply after all outstanding payments are cleared.
